App Features

Awesome Features

Temperature Control

Each sub-container adjusts the ideal temperature to avoid unnecessary food spoilage or freezing.

Humidity Monitoring

Sensors that maintain the proper humidity level, protecting food from dehydration or excess moisture.

IoT Connectivity

Control DittoBox from anywhere through a mobile app, with real-time alerts on food conditions.

Analysis and Reporting

Receive detailed reports and personalized recommendations to improve the efficiency of food preservation and use.

Preventive Maintenance Alert

The application automatically notifies you when the container or any of its components require preventive maintenance, helping to ensure optimal performance and prolonging the life of the device.

Multi-User Access

Allows different restaurant users, such as chefs or warehouse managers, to manage monitoring and parameter settings from the same account, ensuring that the entire team is aware of the preservation conditions.
